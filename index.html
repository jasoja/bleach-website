Enter the Soul Society

Bleach is a popular anime and manga series created by Tite Kubo known for its extensive cast of characters who possess unique abilities called Zanpakutō. The Bankai (卍解, Final Release) is the second and final upgraded form of a Zanpakutō. This is a showcase guiding you through all of the Bankai seen in the anime. Enjoy.
